Layers, Layers, Layers

If you know anything about fall, you know it is all pumpkin spice lattes, Ugg Boots and corn mazes. Fall is one of the best seasons for fashion statements. There are so many different staple items you need to have in your closet for this season.

  • Boots
  • Plaid
  • Denim
  • Scarves
  • Jackets
  • Hats

Layers are so important when it comes to fall weather because the temperature flunctuates so much! It is nice to be able to take your sweater off and still have a cute shirt that coordinates with the rest of your outfit underneath.
